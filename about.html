About Whisper

Whisper is a secure messaging application designed with your privacy as its utmost priority. We believe in the fundamental right to private communication, and our platform is built from the ground up to ensure your messages remain confidential and ephemeral.

Our mission is to provide a simple, intuitive, and highly secure way for individuals to share sensitive information without leaving a trace. Whether it's a personal note, a confidential business detail, or any information you wish to keep private, Whisper offers peace of mind.

How It Works

Whisper employs end-to-end encryption directly in your browser. Here's a simplified overview of the process:

  • Message Creation: When you type a message, it is encrypted on your device using strong AES-256 GCM encryption before it ever leaves your computer. A unique encryption key is generated for each message.
  • Secure Link: A unique, unguessable link is generated. This link contains a token to retrieve the encrypted message and the decryption key (stored in the URL fragment, which is never sent to the server).
  • Storage: Only the encrypted version of your message is stored on our servers. We cannot read your messages, and neither can anyone else who might gain access to our servers.
  • Retrieval & Decryption: When the recipient opens the link, their browser fetches the encrypted message. The decryption key from the URL fragment is then used by their browser to decrypt and display the message.
  • Self-Destruction: Messages are designed to be ephemeral. If "one-time view" is selected, the message is permanently deleted from our servers after the first view. All messages are also deleted after their specified expiration time.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Privacy isn't just a feature at Whisper; it's the core principle. We are committed to:

  • Zero-Knowledge Encryption: We design our systems so that we, the service providers, have zero knowledge of the content of your messages.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We collect the absolute minimum data necessary to provide the service. We do not track users or profile their activity.
  • Transparency: We aim to be transparent about how our service works and how we protect your data. (This section will link to our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service).
  • Data Security: While the content is end-to-end encrypted, we also employ robust security measures to protect the encrypted data and our infrastructure.
